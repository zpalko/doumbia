Facts About World War 1

What is World War 1?

The First World War (or the Great War) was a conflict between the Triple Entente (Great Britain, France, and Russian Empire) against the Triple Alliance (German Empire, Austro-Hungarian Empire, and the Italian Empire). The fighting started in August 1914 and lasted until November 11th, 1918.

What does this Site Contain?

This site is to give very surface-level knowledge on the Great War to people that may want a general understanding with the following:

  1. Events/ Battles that are Important to Know
  2. Military and Technological Innovations
  3. People, Places, and Ideas that made Significant Changes in History
